<p><p>The amino resin market comprises three primary types: Urea Formaldehyde, Melamine Formaldehyde, and Melamine Urea Formaldehyde. Urea Formaldehyde resin is widely used in adhesives and coatings due to its fast curing and cost-effectiveness. Melamine Formaldehyde offers enhanced thermal stability and moisture resistance, making it suitable for high-performance applications. Melamine Urea Formaldehyde combines properties of both, balancing cost and performance, and is commonly used in furniture and decorative laminates, providing a durable finish with improved aesthetics.</p></p>

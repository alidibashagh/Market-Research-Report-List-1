<p><p>The Bag-on-Valve (BOV) packaging market includes several types: Aerosol BOVs, Standard BOVs, and Non-spray/Low-pressure BOVs. Aerosol BOVs utilize pressurized gas to dispense products like creams and sprays, ensuring even distribution. Standard BOVs cover a broader range of applications, supporting both liquid and semi-solid formulations. Non-spray/Low-pressure BOVs operate at reduced pressure, ideal for thicker products such as gels or lotions, allowing for controlled dispensing. Together, these types provide efficient and environmentally-friendly packaging solutions across various industries.</p></p>

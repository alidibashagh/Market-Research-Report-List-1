<p><p>The C-Peptide Test Kit market comprises various testing methodologies, primarily including Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA) and Time-resolved Immunoassay (TRIA). Chemiluminescence Immunoassay utilizes light-emitting reactions to measure C-peptide levels, offering high sensitivity and rapid results, making it suitable for clinical diagnostics. In contrast, Time-resolved Immunoassay employs time-dependent detection of antigens or antibodies, enhancing specificity and sensitivity. Both methods are crucial for diabetes management, enabling accurate assessment of insulin production and monitoring of metabolic disorders.</p></p>

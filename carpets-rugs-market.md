<p><p>The carpets and rugs market comprises various types, each defined by their manufacturing techniques. Tufted carpets are created by punching yarn into a backing material, allowing for diverse designs. Woven rugs involve interlacing fibers on looms, resulting in durability and intricate patterns. Needle-punched rugs are made by matting fibers together with barbed needles, often used for industrial applications. Knotted rugs feature hand-tied knots for intricate and decorative designs. Other types include flatweaves and shag rugs, offering additional style options.</p></p>

<p><p>The OTR (Off-the-Road) Tires market comprises three main types: Radial Tires, Bias Tires, and Solid Tires. Radial Tires feature a construction that allows for better traction and stability, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. Bias Tires have layers that crisscross, providing durability but less flexibility, suitable for rough terrains. Solid Tires, made from a solid rubber composition, eliminate the risk of punctures and are commonly used in forklifts and construction equipment, offering excellent load-bearing capabilities.</p></p>

<p><p>The primary battery recycling market is categorized into two main types: wet primary battery recycling and dry primary battery recycling. Wet primary battery recycling involves the use of aqueous solutions to extract valuable materials from spent batteries, often resulting in higher recovery rates and reduced emissions. In contrast, dry primary battery recycling relies on mechanical processes to segregate and recover materials without the use of liquids, typically offering a more energy-efficient approach. Both methods aim to enhance resource recovery and minimize environmental impact.</p></p>
